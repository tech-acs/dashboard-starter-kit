---
type: report
title: Cases Outside Enumeration Duration
description: Records enumerated outside the expected exercise duration
---

Lists household records where the enumeration timestamp falls outside the expected
exercise start and end dates.

Queries for records where the enumeration date is before the official start date or
after the official end date. Report should display household ID, EA code,
enumeration timestamp, enumerator ID, and the expected date range.

Renders as a table for data quality review, flagging potential data integrity issues.
