---
type: report
title: Duplicate Cases
description: Listing of duplicate enumeration records
---

Generates a report listing duplicate household or person records identified during enumeration.

Queries the enumerated records for duplicate entries based on key identifiers such as
address, household ID, or person details within each enumeration area. The report should
flag records with matching identifiers across multiple enumeration submissions.

Renders as a downloadable table or CSV with columns for EA code, duplicate identifiers,
and record details.
