---
type: report
title: EAs Accomplishing Assigned Targets
description: Enumeration areas that met or exceeded their assigned household targets
---

Lists enumeration areas (EAs) that successfully accomplished their assigned household
enumeration targets.

Calculated by comparing the number of households enumerated in each EA against the
EA's assigned target. Includes EAs that met or exceeded the target. Report should
display EA code, target count, actual count, and percentage achieved.

Renders as a table with accomplishment status and can be filtered by administrative area.
