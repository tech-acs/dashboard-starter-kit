title: EAs That Started Enumeration
description: Enumeration areas that have begun the enumeration process
---

Lists enumeration areas (EAs) that have started the household enumeration exercise.

Identified by EAs with at least one enumerated household record in the system.
Report should display EA code, date of first enumeration, total households enumerated
so far, and administrative area hierarchy.

Renders as a table sorted by start date, showing progress status for each EA.
