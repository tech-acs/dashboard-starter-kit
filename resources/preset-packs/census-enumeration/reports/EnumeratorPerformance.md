---
type: report
title: Enumerator Performance
description: Performance summary of enumerators across enumeration areas
---

Provides a performance summary for each enumerator based on their assigned EAs.

Metrics include: households enumerated, completion rate against target, average daily
enumeration rate, and number of EAs managed. Report should be grouped by enumerator
and include their assigned administrative areas.

Renders as a table sorted by completion rate, with the ability to drill down into
each enumerator's EA-level details.
