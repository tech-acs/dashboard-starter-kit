---
type: report
title: Partial Cases
description: Households with incomplete enumeration data
---

Lists households where the enumeration was started but not completed, resulting in
partial data.

Identified by records with missing required fields or incomplete status flags. Report
should display household ID, EA code, enumerator ID, the last completed section,
and reason for partial status (if available).

Renders as a table for supervisory follow-up to ensure complete enumeration coverage.
