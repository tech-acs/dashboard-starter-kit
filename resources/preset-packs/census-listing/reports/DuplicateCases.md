---
type: report
title: Duplicate Cases
description: Listing of duplicate structure or household records
---

Generates a report listing duplicate structure or household records identified during listing.

Queries the listed records for duplicate entries based on key identifiers such as address or structure ID within each enumeration area. The report should flag records with matching identifiers across multiple listing submissions.

Renders as a downloadable table or CSV with columns for EA code, duplicate identifiers, and record details.
