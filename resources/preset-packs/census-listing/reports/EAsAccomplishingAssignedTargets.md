---
type: report
title: EAs Accomplishing Assigned Targets
description: Enumeration areas that met or exceeded their assigned listing targets
---

Lists enumeration areas (EAs) that successfully accomplished their assigned listing targets.

Calculated by comparing the number of structures listed in each EA against the EA's assigned target. Includes EAs that met or exceeded the target. Report should display EA code, target count, actual count, and percentage achieved.

Renders as a table with accomplishment status and can be filtered by administrative area.
