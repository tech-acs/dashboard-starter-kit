title: EAs That Started Listing
description: Enumeration areas that have begun the listing exercise
---

Lists enumeration areas (EAs) that have started the listing exercise.

Identified by EAs with at least one listed structure record in the system. Report should display EA code, date of first listing, total structures listed so far, and administrative area hierarchy.

Renders as a table sorted by start date, showing progress status for each EA.
