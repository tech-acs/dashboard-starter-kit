---
type: report
title: Enumerator Performance
description: Performance summary of enumerators across their assigned listing areas
---

Provides a performance summary for each enumerator based on their assigned listing areas.

Metrics include: structures listed, completion rate against target, average daily listing rate, and number of EAs managed. Report should be grouped by enumerator and include their assigned administrative areas.

Renders as a table sorted by completion rate, with the ability to drill down into each enumerator's EA-level details.
