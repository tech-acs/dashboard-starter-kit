---
type: report
title: Households Above Size Threshold
description: Households with more than 50 members flagged for review during listing
---

Lists households where the recorded household size exceeds the maximum threshold of 50 members, flagged for data quality review during listing.

Queries household records where `total_household_members` exceeds 50. Report should display household ID, EA code, recorded household size, enumerator ID, and administrative area. These cases may indicate data entry errors.

Renders as a downloadable table for supervisory review and verification.
