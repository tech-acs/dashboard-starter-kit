---
type: report
title: Partial Cases
description: Structures or households with incomplete listing data
---

Lists structures or households where the listing was started but not completed, resulting in partial data.

Identified by records with missing required fields or incomplete status flags. Report should display structure/household ID, EA code, enumerator ID, the last completed section, and reason for partial status (if available).

Renders as a table for supervisory follow-up to ensure complete listing coverage.
